--- layout: home title: River Hill SciOly Invitational subtitle: March 2nd - March 9th, 2024 --- Welcome to the second-ever River Hill Division C Science Olympiad Invitational!

RHSOI (River Hill Science Olympiad Invitational) provides a vital opportunity for teams to sharpen their competitive edge before regional and state-level science competitions. Designed as a strategic precursor to these events, RHSOI offers meticulously crafted tests authored by seasoned Division C competitors and Science Olympiad alumni from across the nation.
Participants benefit not only from the rigorous challenges presented but also from the feedback and guidance provided by experienced organizers. Beyond the competitive aspect, RHSOI fosters a sense of community among aspiring young scientists, facilitating the exchange of ideas and experiences.
